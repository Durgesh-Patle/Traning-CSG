Digital Slate

A digital slate is a paperless writing and drawing device that uses a pressure-sensitive LCD screen and a stylus or pen. Digital slates can be used for a variety of purposes, including:

  • Learning: Kids can use digital slates to practice handwriting, spelling, and arithmetic <
  • Notetaking: Adults can use digital slates to take notes or jot down reminders.
  • Sketching: Digital slates can be used to make quick sketches or drawings.
  • Design: Designers can use digital slates to save their ideas and drafts
